# TU Delft V3 LEI kite benchmark data

Open data (CC-BY) from the TU Delft Airborne Wind Energy group.
Repository: https://github.com/awegroup/TUDELFT_V3_KITE
Dataset index: https://awegroup.github.io/TUDELFT_V3_KITE/docs/datasets.html

Files here (downloaded from raw.githubusercontent.com):
- WindTunnel_Re5e5_alpha_sweep_beta_0_Poland2025.csv
- CFD_RANS_Re1e6_alpha_sweep_beta_0_Vire2022_CorrectedByPoland2025.csv
- CFD_RANS_Re5e5_alpha_sweep_beta_0_Vire2020_CorrectedByPoland2025.csv
- properties.yaml

Cite:
- Poland, J.A.W., van Spronsen, J.M., Gaunaa, M., Schmehl, R. (2026).
  Wind tunnel load measurements of a leading-edge inflatable kite rigid-scale
  model. Wind Energy Science 11, 911. doi:10.5194/wes-11-911-2026
- Viré, A., Lebesque, G., Folkersma, M., Schmehl, R. (2022). Effect of
  Chordwise Struts and Misaligned Flow on the Aerodynamic Performance of a
  Leading-Edge Inflatable Wing. Energies 15, 1450. doi:10.3390/en15041450
- Viré, A., Demkowicz, P., Folkersma, M., Roullier, A., Schmehl, R. (2020).
  RANS simulations of the flow past a LEI wing for AWE. J. Phys. Conf. Ser.
  1618, 032007.

Note: the 25 m² V3 is a crosswind-optimised AWE traction kite. Its polars are
the best open LEI reference available, but transfer to large static lifters
with the caveats noted in reports/l0.md.
